Statements of object and Reason


There have been demands for inclusion of certain languages in the Eighth Schedule to the Constitution. It is proposed to include Bodo language in the Eighth Schedule to the Constitution.


Provision under Amendment


  • The amendment encourages the inclusion of Bodo, Dogri, Maithili, and Santhali into the constitution’s 8th Schedule. The number of languages in the VIIIth Schedule ascends to 22 with the inclusion of these four languages.
