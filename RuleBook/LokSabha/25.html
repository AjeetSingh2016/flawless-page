CHAPTER XXIV


COMMUNICATIONS BETWEEN PRESIDENT AND THE HOUSE


Communications from President to House


Rule 246. Communications from the President to the House shall be made to the Speaker by written message signed by the President or, if the President is absent from the place of sitting of the House, the message shall be conveyed to the Speaker through a Minister.
